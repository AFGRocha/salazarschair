About

Salazar's Chair is a videogame done for the Game Development class in the course of Web Technologies and Information Systems.

The game is a parody of the concept that Salazar, from the Estado Novo regime from 1933 to 1974, died by falling from his chair.

In this game you control the chair that Salazar fell asleep on, the objective is to explore the room and find ways to kill Salazar, but be carefull to not wake him up!
